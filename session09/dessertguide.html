Mochi

Soft and chewy rice cake, slightly sweet. Sometimes filled with red bean paste or ice cream.

Macaron

Light and crunchy on the outside, soft inside. Sweet and delicate flavors like chocolate, raspberry, or pistachio.

Tiramisu

Creamy and smooth with coffee flavor. Light, sweet, and slightly bitter from cocoa.

Red Bean Soup

Sweet and warm dessert soup made from red beans. Soft and smooth with a gentle sweetness, often served with small rice balls.

Brownie

Chocolatey and chewy, slightly crunchy on top.
